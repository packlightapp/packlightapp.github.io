Privacy Policy for PackLight

Effective Date: 3/3/2025

This page outlines PackLight's policies with regards to the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

In short, no personally identifiable information (PII) is collected and any usage metrics collected are anonymized.

1. Information Collected

  • No Personal Information: The app does not collect personally identifiable information. No name, email, or any other PII is gathered.
  • Usage Data: Information about how the app is used (such as the features accessed and how often they are used) may be collected. This data is anonymized and does not identify you personally.
  • Device Information: The app may collect non-personally identifiable information such as device type and operating system for the purpose of improving the app.
  • Locally Stored Data: App data is stored locally on your device. The app will not share data anywhere unless you explicitly choose to post a packing list to packlightapp.com (see below).

2. How the Information Is Used

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the app
  • To understand typical usage patterns and identify highly utilized features

3. Sharing Data to packlightapp.com

  • Public Sharing: Users have the option to share packing list data publicly on packlightapp.com. Any data posted will be visible to others.
  • CloudKit Storage: Publicly shared data is stored in iCloud according to Apple's policies. This data is only what the user chooses to share and is not personally identifiable.

4. Anonymized Usage Metrics

Any usage data collected (such as how often you use the app or which features are accessed) is fully anonymized. This data is sent to third-party analytics providers to help improve the app. It does not contain any personally identifiable information.

5. Changes to This Policy

The privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Effective Date".

6. Contact Information

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact packlightapp@gmail.com.
